# Vertical Drama / Microdrama Industry Brief
## For CastBrief Outreach Campaign

---

## Market Overview
- Global microdrama revenues projected to reach **$26B by 2030**
- US revenues: **$819M in 2024**, projected **$3.8B by 2030**
- China market: **$7B in 2024** (surpassing theatrical box office)
- **30-40 vertical productions per month in LA alone**
- Typical budget: **$100K-$300K per title**, shot in **6-12 days**
- SAG-AFTRA launched **Verticals Agreement** (Oct 2025) for productions under $300K
