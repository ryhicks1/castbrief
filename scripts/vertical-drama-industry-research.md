# Vertical Short-Form Drama / Microdrama Industry Research
## Compiled March 24, 2026

---

## MARKET OVERVIEW

- Global microdrama revenue: ~$11B in 2025 (Omdia), projected $22-26B by 2030
- China accounts for ~83% of revenue ($9.4B in 2025); U.S. is #2 at ~9% and growing fast
- U.S. market: $819M in 2024, projected $3.8B by 2030
- 830M+ viewers consume microdramas in China; 60% paying/transacting
- Format: 1-3 minute episodes, vertical (9x16), 20-100 episodes per series
- Typical budget: $100K-$300K per series, 7-10 day shoots

- **Parent:** Crazy Maple Studio (subsidiary of COL Group, Beijing-based digital publisher listed on Shenzhen exchange)
- **Key Executives:**

## HOLLYWOOD STUDIOS & NEW ENTRANTS

### 16. Holywater (MyDrama) + Fox Entertainment
- **What:** Ukrainian tech company. MyDrama is #1 vertical streaming app among American/European companies. 85M+ users across platforms. Fox took equity stake and committed to 200+ titles over 2 years
- **HQ:** Founded in Kyiv, Ukraine. Leadership in Portugal. Production in L.A.
- **Platforms:** My Drama, My Passion (books), My Muse (AI-generated), FreeBits (AVOD)
- **Key Executives:**
  - **Bogdan Nesvit** - Co-Founder & Co-CEO
  - **Anatolii Kasianov** - Co-Founder & Co-CEO
  - **Oleksandra Naidonova** - Brand & PR Lead
- **Fox Entertainment Executives on the deal:**
  - **Rob Wade** - CEO, Fox Entertainment
  - **Prentiss Fraser** - President, Fox Entertainment Global
- **Funding:** $22M Series A (largest microdrama investment outside Asia). Led by Horizon Capital, with Endeavor Catalyst and Wheelhouse
